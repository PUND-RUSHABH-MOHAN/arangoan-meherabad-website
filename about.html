Arangaon, also known as Meherabad, is a village in Ahilyanagar district of Maharashtra, India. Located along the banks of the Godavari basin region, Arangaon is home to a close-knit farming community that has preserved its traditions across generations. The village is internationally recognized as Meherabad — the spiritual centre established by Avatar Meher Baba, which draws thousands of pilgrims and visitors from around the world every year.

The Grampanchayat is the local self-government body responsible for the administration and development of the village. Our office — Gramsansad Bhavan — serves as the centre for citizen services, public meetings, and community decisions. We focus on inclusive growth, modern infrastructure, and preserving the cultural identity that makes Arangaon special.

Our Mission

To deliver responsive, transparent and inclusive local self-government — improving the quality of life for every resident of Arangaon while preserving our cultural and spiritual heritage for future generations.

Our Values

  • Transparency in every decision and public expenditure
  • Equality and dignity for all citizens regardless of background
  • Sustainable development and clean environment
  • Respect for our cultural and spiritual heritage
  • Inclusive governance — giving every resident a voice
